Facilities

The Summit Carousel

Looking for a thrill?!

Take a spin on The Summit Carousel, where classic charm meets breathtaking views. Located at the highest point of Central Park, this hand-carved carousel offers a delightful ride for all ages. Enjoy scenic vistas while riding beautifully crafted animals in a family-friendly atmosphere. A must-do for visitors seeking fun and nostalgia!

The Alpine Rush Water Slide

Get your adrenaline flowing on The Alpine Rush Water Slide! Zoom down the winding slopes and feel the thrill as you race through refreshing mountain streams. Set against stunning alpine scenery, this high-speed slide is perfect for water lovers seeking adventure and cool fun. Grab your friends, slide into excitement, and make a splash at Central Park!

The Horizon Wheel

Take your adventure to new heights on The Horizon Wheel! This towering observation ride offers stunning 360-degree views of Central Park's rolling hills, forested valleys, and distant peaks. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful escape or a scenic photo op, the Horizon Wheel is the perfect way to see the park from above. A thrilling and relaxing experience for all ages!

The Cave Racer Roller Coaster

Get ready for an underground thrill on The Cave Racer Roller Coaster! Zoom through dark tunnels and twist around sharp turns as you race beneath the mountain’s surface. With heart-pounding drops and unexpected loops, this coaster delivers the perfect mix of excitement and adventure. Hold on tight – the Cave Racer is a ride you won’t forget!

Legends of the Peaks

Step into the world of Legends of the Peaks, where the stories of mountain explorers, ancient cultures, and park legends come to life! Meet the vibrant characters who have shaped the history of Central Park through interactive exhibits and captivating storytelling. Discover the rich traditions, myths, and adventures that make this mountain a place of wonder.
